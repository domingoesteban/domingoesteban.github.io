Bio

Domingo Esteban

Domingo Esteban is an engineer and researcher in Robotics. Since 2013, he is a collaborator in the Robotics Lab of the Universidad Carlos III de Madrid in Spain.

Also since 2013, he is working in the Robotics division of IXION Industry and Aerospace.

In 2014, he received his M.Sc. degree in Robotics and Automation from the Universidad Carlos III de Madrid. In 2009, he received the B.Sc. degree in Industrial Engineering from the Universidad Nacional de San Agustín and, in 2010, his B.Sc. in Economics and Business from the Universidad Católica San Pablo, both in Arequipa-Peru.

In Peru, he has worked in the mining industry for two of the biggest companies in the country.

While in Huaraz, Esteban can witness the inequality of Peruvian society and the lack of opportunities to enable them to pull through. Aware of the positive impact that technology can have, Esteban’s goal is to give everyone access to that new technology that allows them to improve their quality of life.
